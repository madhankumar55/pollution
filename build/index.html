Pollution
What is pollution?

Pollution is the contamination of the environment by harmful substances or pollutants,
which can negatively impact the health of living organisms, ecosystems, and the planet as a whole.
It occurs when pollutants—such as chemicals, waste, noise, heat, or light—are introduced into the natural environment at levels that exceed its ability to absorb, degrade, or manage them.
